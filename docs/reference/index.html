Connect to WiFi & sign into laptopSign in via personal Microsoft account on first boot
Set up Gmail — firstname@digitalnexa.comSend a test email to the Operations Manager before emailing any client
Download Slack & sign in with NEXA credentialsJoin #general, #your-department, and #nexa-social on Day 1
Access Google Drivedrive.google.com — NEVER save client files locally
Log into People HR & complete your profileAll leave requests go through People HR — not Slack or email

Meeting standards
How NEXA runs meetings — and decides when not to have one
Do
  • Send an agenda at least 24 hours before.
  • Default to 30 minutes — start on time, end 5 min early.
  • Capture action items + owners + dates before closing.
  • Post minutes to the shared Drive folder within 24 hours.
  • Decline an invite that has no agenda.
Don't
  • Book a meeting when a Slack thread would do.
  • Invite more than 6 people unless absolutely necessary.
  • Use back-to-back hours — leave 5–10 min buffers.
  • Run over time — push to async or schedule a follow-up.
  • Skip minutes for "quick" meetings — write them anyway.

Status update only?Async note
Client-sensitive?Email + Meet
